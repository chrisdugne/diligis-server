￼#FAQ

How does it work?

It’s simple. Sign in with your LinkedIn account. Add the details of your forthcoming trips. Receive notifications when other users have similar arrangements. Review their profile. Contact your selection and arrange to meet at the airport or at your destination.

What if I don’t travel that often?

The purpose of Diligis is to help you optimize your time when you are travelling. If you take a trip to meet one customer, whether because meeting them helps retaining their loyalty or winning them over, you’ll spend time in transportation and maybe you’ll stay overnight. This time you spend in a flight or at a destination can be better used if you arrange to meet with other professionals that you know in advance have the right profile. This is true whether your travel a lot or only occasionally.

How do I find out who best to meet?

As soon as you upload a planned trip, we match your travel arrangements against those of other users. When there’s a match, you get a notification and you can visit the users’ profile. From there, you can decide whether it is worth contacting them.

How do I contact other users?

At Diligis, you can only contact users that share similar arrangements at any one time. This protects them and you from spam. Every notification you receive is an opportunity to contact users that will use the same transportation or will be at the destination or event as you will. Diligis provides you with a mailbox so you can send and receive direct messages.

Can I contact other users?

Diligis is a business development tool for traveling professionals. The point is that you develop business with the right people when traveling.

Can I stay in contact with users I previously messaged or met?

Well, if you met on a trip, chances are you exchanged contact information. Diligis is not a social network. Whilst you find other users online, you actually meet offline.
